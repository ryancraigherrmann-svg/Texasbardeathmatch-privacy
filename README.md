# Privacy Policy — Texas Bar Deathmatch

**Last updated: April 20, 2026**

Texas Bar Deathmatch does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All bar sales data displayed in the app comes from the Texas Comptroller's publicly available Mixed Beverage Gross Receipts dataset. No user accounts, analytics, or tracking are used.

The app caches public TABC data locally on your device to improve performance. This data never leaves your device.
